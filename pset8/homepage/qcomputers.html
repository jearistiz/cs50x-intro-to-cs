Quantum Computers around the world

In the last years there has been a big competition in the race of building the first fully functional quantum computer. Companies such as IBM, Google and Microsoft, amongst others, are right now building their own Quantum Computers. The fully functional quantum computer hasn't been built yet because more advanced Quantum Error Correction protocols are needed in order to overcome several types of noise present in Quantum Hardwares. However, the mentioned companies do have partially-functional Quantum Computers, some of which we introduce here.
